## Full Description

### Overview
Hanuman Chalisa App is your personal devotional companion for the sacred Hanuman Chalisa prayer. Designed for spiritual practice, this app makes it easy to chant anytime, anywhere—even without an internet connection.

### Key Features

**🎵 High-Quality Audio**
- Crystal-clear male and female voice recitations
- Seamlessly synced with timestamped lyrics
- Play, pause, and seek through the entire prayer
- Lock screen controls for convenience

**📖 Bilingual Lyrics**
- View Hindi lyrics as you listen
- Follow the recitation word-by-word with precise timestamps
- Language toggle for enhanced learning

**🔥 Progress Tracking**
- Build your daily streak—consistency matters
- Weekly and all-time statistics
- Visual heatmap of your practice history
- Track completion counts and personal best streaks

**💾 Works Offline**
- Complete prayer experience without internet
- All audio and lyrics loaded locally
- Uninterrupted spiritual practice anywhere

**👥 Community & Leaderboard**
- Sign in with Google to sync progress across devices
- View community leaderboards (weekly and all-time)
- See how others are building their practice

**⚙️ Customizable Settings**
- Adjust text size for comfortable reading
- Customize playback speed to match your pace
- Enable/disable notifications for daily reminders
- Choose your preferred voice (male/female)

**🔔 Daily Reminders**
- Set custom reminder notifications
- Never miss a day of practice

### Why Use Hanuman Chalisa App?
The Hanuman Chalisa is a 40-verse devotional hymn praising Lord Hanuman. Traditionally chanted for spiritual strength, courage, and devotion. This app removes barriers to daily practice—no need for physical books or internet, just your phone.

Perfect for:
- Daily spiritual practice
- Learning the prayer and its meaning
- Building a consistent meditation/chanting habit
- Community engagement with fellow devotees

### Privacy & Data
- Your practice data is stored locally on your device
- Optional Google Sign-In to sync progress to the cloud
- Full control over your data
- No ads, no tracking, no invasive permissions
