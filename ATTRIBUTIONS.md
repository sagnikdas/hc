## Hanuman Chalisa Historical Context

The **Hanuman Chalisa** is a revered Hindu devotional hymn:
- **Composed**: Circa 1680 CE
- **Poet**: Maharishi Tulsidas
- **Original**: 40 verses (chalisa = 40)
- **Language**: Awadhi / Brajbhasha
- **Devotee**: Dedicated to Lord Hanuman (monkey deity in Hindu mythology)
- **Purpose**: Devotional worship and spiritual meditation

The prayer is chanted by millions of devotees worldwide and is considered one of the most important Hanuman prayers in Hinduism.
