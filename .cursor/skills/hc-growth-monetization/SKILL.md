# HC Growth and Monetization

## Goal

Improve conversion and retention without interrupting prayer experience.

## Priority Sequence

1. Core value first (playback and streak).
2. High-intent paywall triggers.
3. Referral and sharing loops.
4. Pricing and copy experiments.

## High-Intent Triggers

- After 3 completed sessions in one day.
- At milestones (11/21/51).
- On premium feature intent tap.
- Weekly progress recap CTA.

## Guardrails

- Never interrupt active chanting.
- Max one proactive paywall/day.
- Always provide dismiss option.
- Keep free tier meaningful.

## Metrics

- Paywall view to subscribe conversion.
- D1/D7 retention.
- Weekly active users.
- Invite conversion rate.
- Subscription churn.
